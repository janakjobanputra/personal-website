Meta

Meta owns four of the largest social media platforms, each with over one billion monthly active users.

I joined Meta as the all up design lead for the Facebook Incubator. I oversaw design for all Incubator products, developed bespoke 0-1 strategies, evangelized best practices, and mentored colleagues from all disciplines. I worked vertically with pillars to identify, validate, and select new opportunities for incubation. I worked horizontally with the design leadership council to identify, staff, bootstrap, and ship the highest priority efforts across the company. I led two company-wide surge efforts, was responsible for multiple sprints, and participated in a hackathon. I contributed to 100% increases in both design system adoption and quality.

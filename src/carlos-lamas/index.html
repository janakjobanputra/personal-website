Carlos Lamas

Carlos is an accomplished freelance video editor, based in Minneapolis.

In 2012, I was contracted by Carlos Lamas as he was preparing to break out on his own. He was looking for a brand he could work under and a portfolio site to both showcase his work and build his customer base.

Website

  • Principal Designer
  • Front-End Developer

Carlos wanted a simple site where his work was the focus. He was also keen on maintaining the site himself. I designed a glorified video player backed by Vimeo which can be updated via semantic HTML that auto-deploys to GitHub Pages. The website launched his freelance career and established him as one of the premier video editors in the Twin Cities. Carlos loves purple.
